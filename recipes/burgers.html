BBQ Burger Recipe

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Total Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 burger patties

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Directions

Step 1

Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat
and lightly oil grate.

Step 2

Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together
in medium bowl.

Step 3

Add ground beef and bread crumbs;
mix with hands or fork until well blended.

Step 4

Form in four 3/4-inch patties.

Step 5

Place patties on the grill. Cover and cook for about
6 to 8 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into
the ceneter should read at least 160-degrees.

Step 6

Serve hot and enjoy!
