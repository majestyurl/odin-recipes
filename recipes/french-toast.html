French Toast Recipe

French Toast

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Total Time: 15 mins
  • Servings: 3
  • Yield: 6 Slices

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • salt to taste
  • 6 thick slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Directions:

Step 1:

Gather all of the ingredients.

Step 2:

Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon,
and salt together in a shallow bowl.

Step 3:

Lightly butter a griddle or skillet
and heat over medium-high heat.
Dunk bread in the egg mixture,
soaking both sides.
