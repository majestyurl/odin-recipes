Smoothie Recipes

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Total Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Place bananas, milk, honey, peanut butter
and ice cubes in the blender.

Step 3

Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.

Step 4

Enjoy!
